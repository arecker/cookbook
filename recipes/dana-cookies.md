## Materials

- butter (3 sticks)
- brown sugar (1 box or 2.25 cups)

- eggs (2)
- vanilla extract (2 tsp)

- flour (2 cups)
- table salt (2 tsp)
- baking soda (1 tsp)
- oatmeal, raw (18 oz or 6 cups)
- dark chocolate chips (1 bag or 10 oz)
- butterscotch chips (1 bag or 11 oz)
